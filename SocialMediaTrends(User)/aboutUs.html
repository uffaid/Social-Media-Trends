About Us

Social Media Trends is a search tool that helps in search of the gathered data from online social media like Facebook, Twitter, Newsletters and Blogs and performing elementary analysis over the gathered data and putting them in certain standard format according to various aspects. It helps in portraying the mood of the people by analyzing “Posts” and “Tweets” of people over certain geographical area and generating visuals accordingly. By analyzing the messages posted by millions of people on Social Media sites one can discover the fluctuating emotional tone of people throughout the day or week.It can be helpful for Researchers also they can see whether the mood improves or deteriorates as the day progresses.Successful implementation of this application will allow a user in understanding people's mood for a social incident and helps to look into the general trends of the people's opinions by multiple number of search criteria’s. The type of visualization this tool provides will be very easy to understand and will be used by the user for various research purposes. Area wise and topic wise visualization of the trending topic will be incorporated into this tool using visuals like geo maps, Line/Area, bar plots, scatter graphs.
